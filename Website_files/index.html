Welcome to my story website, MQTA (Monkey Quest Team Alpha). This story was made for the memory of the great game, that personally I loved very much, Monkey Quest.
    If you never heard of this game, you can read the Legend Of Monkey Quest

    In this story, six brave monkeys who survived a huge explosion that killed most of ook's monkeys, would fight a shadow demon, who was blown up by the monkey king centuries ago. Its name is Ka. The heroes of this story are: The leader - Hooper Crookedtrip (me), The Chim Foo fighter - Flash Greenblast, The Ootu Mystic alchemist - Sebastien Forestlotus, The Sea Dragon pirate - Harvy Bizzybreaker, The Ice Raider Viking - Carmen Breezyheart, and the last, The Mek Tek mechanic - Sonic Secretorama.
    Will they beat Ka? Will they survive? Find out by reading the episodes of this awesome story! Hope you enjoy it!! :)
